📜 Terms & Policy

Referral Program Rules

  • Each new user receives a $5 bonus upon successful registration.
  • Referrers earn $2.00 for every valid referral that completes registration.
  • Self-referrals or fraudulent accounts will be permanently banned.
  • Multiple accounts per person are strictly prohibited.
  • RefEarn reserves the right to reverse fraudulent earnings.

Withdrawal Policy

Users may request a withdrawal once their account balance reaches the minimum threshold. Withdrawals are reviewed and processed within 24–72 business hours.

  • Supported methods: EasyPaisa, UPI, and USDT (BEP20 Network).
  • Processing time: 24–72 hours after admin approval.
  • RefEarn may request identity verification before processing large withdrawals.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the minimum information required to operate the platform (name, email, payment details). Your data is never sold to third parties. All payment information is encrypted and stored securely.

ℹ️ About RefEarn

What is RefEarn?

RefEarn is a referral-based earning platform that allows anyone to generate income by simply sharing their unique invite code with friends, family, and social networks.

We believe everyone deserves access to fair, transparent, and easy-to-understand earning opportunities. No complicated tasks. No hidden catches. Just share, invite, and earn.

Our Mission

To build the most trusted and transparent referral earning platform, empowering users worldwide to generate real, withdrawable income through the power of community sharing.
